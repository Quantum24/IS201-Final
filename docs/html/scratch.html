Resorts I Have Skied

I have skied these resort areas: Stevens Pass, Whistler, Crystal Mountain, Snowbird, Snowbasin, Alta, Solitude, Mission Ridge, Brighton, Park City, and Deer Valley.

My Top 3 Ski Areas

  1. Whistler
    • My #1 for huge terrain and true destination skiing vibes
    • Endless variety: bowls, trees, groomers, and alpine routes
  2. Snowbird
    • My #2 for amazing powder and steep terrain
    • Great technical runs and long laps when conditions are good
  3. Stevens Pass
    • My #3 because it has been my home resort for years
    • I know the mountain well and always have fun there
